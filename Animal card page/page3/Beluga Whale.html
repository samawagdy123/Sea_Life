Beluga Whale

DISTRIBUTION

Restricted to subpolar and polar waters above the Arctic Circle

ECOSYSTEM/HABITAT

Shallow, coastal waters and near the ice edge; sometimes in rivers

FEEDING HABITS

Foraging predator

TAXONOMY

Suborder Odontoceti (toothed whales), Family Monodontidae (white whales)
