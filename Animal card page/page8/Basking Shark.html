Basking Shark

DISTRIBUTION

Worldwide in warm to cold temperate latitudes; absent from the tropics

ECOSYSTEM/HABITAT

Coastal to open ocean (pelagic)

FEEDING HABITS

Filter feeder

TAXONOMY

Order Lamniformes (mackerel sharks and relatives), Family Cetorhinidae (basking sharks)
